Julia Perczel

Social Anthropologist

I am a social anthropologist interested in how communities negotiate belonging, displacement, and cultural continuity. My research draws on long-term ethnographic fieldwork to explore the intersections of kinship, place, and institutional power.

Based at the University of Oxford, I teach on the MSc in Social Anthropology and contribute to collaborative projects examining heritage practices across Central Europe.
